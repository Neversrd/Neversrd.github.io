About me

I am Huang Li, a fulltime software engineer of Tencent based in Shenzhen, China. Before that, I worked as an ML SWE (Machine Learning Software Engineer) intern at Google Beijing for three months and a research intern at Sensetime Hangzhou for six months.

I received my master degree of Computer Science from Zhejiang University and bachelor degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

My research interests are Computer Graphics (especially rendering and simulation), Machine Learning, Parallel Computing and Software Engineering.

In addition to my day-to-day work, I am exploring photography. Hope I could deliver some impressive and elegant pictures in the future.
